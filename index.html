About the Trust

Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (SAST) was established in 1974. In its journey of commitments to the nation for more than five decades, SAST runs 515 value-based and quality-oriented educational institutions catering to 150,000+ students across the country, imparting education from primary to professional courses. The Trust focuses on providing education in semi-urban and rural areas, operating major institutions in the fields of Medicine, Engineering, Pharmacy, Ayurveda, Management, and other professional courses, including special education centers for destitute, physically handicapped (blind), and mentally challenged individuals.

About the Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering department, established in 1981, has been affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, Karnataka, since 1998. Recognized by AICTE and accredited twice by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), the department offers UG, PG, and research programs, fostering student growth with a strong societal impact.

The department boasts experienced faculty specializing in VLSI, embedded systems, digital communication, microwave and antenna technology, DSP, image processing, computer networks, nano-electronics, and MEMS. Well-equipped laboratories support research and projects with advanced software tools like Matlab, Tanner, LabVIEW, HFSS, Multisim, and Vivado, along with Spectrum and Logic Analyzers.

About the Conference

The International Conference on Emerging Research in Smart Electronics, Communication Technologies, Informatics, and Machine Intelligence serves as a premier platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to present and discuss the latest advancements, innovations, and challenges in these rapidly evolving domains.

This conference aims to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange, focusing on cutting-edge topics such as:

  • Smart electronics and embedded systems.
  • Emerging communication technologies, including IoT and 5G/6G.
  • Advanced informatics for data-driven solutions.
  • Breakthroughs in machine intelligence and AI applications.

ECMI-2025 welcomes contributions in the form of original research papers, innovative prototypes, and industrial case studies. By bringing together experts from academia and industry, the conference aims to inspire groundbreaking research and foster new partnerships.

Participants will have the opportunity to explore emerging trends, engage in interdisciplinary discussions, and contribute to shaping the future of technology and innovation.
